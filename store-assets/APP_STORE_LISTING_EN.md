--- APP DESCRIPTION (main store text, plain text) ---

Subly — Subscription & Expense Tracker

Subly helps you stay on top of recurring subscriptions and day-to-day spending in one clear, fast interface. Your data stays on your device by default—no forced cloud signup to use the app. Optional device sync lives in Settings if you ever need it.

SUBSCRIPTIONS

Add streaming, software, utilities, or any recurring service. Billing cycles include daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly with custom frequency where supported. Pin favorites, use categories, tags, and payment methods, and switch between compact and expanded lists. Group by category or payment method, run batch actions for cleanup, and open details with full payment history. Track auto-renewal, inactive plans, overdue charges, and notes or links so renewals and price changes do not surprise you.

EXPENSES

Record one-time purchases with amounts, categories, tags, notes, and optional links. Filter by period, category, or tags. Export expenses to CSV for spreadsheets. Link expenses to subscriptions when that fits your workflow.

DASHBOARD

Configure which blocks you see and how they are ordered. You can surface overdue and upcoming payments, summary stats, monthly budget progress, savings views, spending trends, forecasts for the next month or quarter, lifetime subscription cost, averages per category, charts for breakdowns and trends, expense summaries, top expenses, average ticket size, spending by weekday, month-over-month comparison, tag-based views, and exchange-rate history when you use multiple currencies.

CALENDAR

See upcoming charges on a calendar. Review monthly totals and jump from a date into the related subscription to plan around paydays and bills.

CURRENCIES

Support for many currencies plus room for custom entries. Refresh exchange rates on a schedule, convert to your base currencies, and use converter presets where it saves time.

NOTIFICATIONS

System notifications for upcoming and overdue payments, timed with billing cycles. Pick morning, evening, or a custom time and customize titles and messages. In-app alerts help you act quickly. Optionally send reminders through Telegram.

SETTINGS AND HOUSEHOLD

Choose from many themes. Manage categories, payment methods, and tags with icons and order. Add household members so spending can be attributed correctly. Set a monthly budget with a clear progress view.

DESKTOP EXTRAS (WHERE AVAILABLE)

On desktop builds you can use the system tray to see what is due soon, open the app to details from the tray, minimize to tray on close, and start Subly with the operating system if you enable it.

YOUR DATA

Export and import Subly archives (file extension .subly), JSON, and CSV, and import from CSV where supported for backup and migration without vendor lock-in.

Optional cloud sync works with providers you already use: iCloud Drive, Google Drive, Dropbox, Microsoft OneDrive, or WebDAV. Sync uses an app-scoped file; when the cloud copy changes elsewhere you can merge or choose how to resolve conflicts. Login-related fields you store for subscriptions use secure storage on the platform and are not dumped into casual exports.

PRIVACY AND LANGUAGES

Subly is local-first: data stays on your device until you turn on sync. The app is available in multiple languages.

PLATFORMS

Subly runs on macOS, Windows, Linux, iOS, and iPadOS. On iPhone you can add a Home Screen widget to see upcoming renewals at a glance when it is set up on your device.
