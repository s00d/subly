# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** May 4, 2026  

This Privacy Policy describes how **Subly** (“**we**”, “**us**”, or “**our**”) handles information when you use the Subly applications, installers, and related materials we publish (including listings in app stores, our open-source repository, documentation, and marketing pages).  

**Subly** is a subscription and expense tracker. Unless stated otherwise, data you enter is processed **primarily on your device**.

---

## 1. Who this policy applies to

This policy applies to anyone who downloads, installs, or uses Subly on any supported platform (for example desktop, mobile, or web builds we distribute), and to visitors of our public project pages and store listings that reference this document.

---

## 2. Information we do not collect by design

- We do **not** operate a mandatory Subly account or central user database for core app use.
- We do **not** sell your personal information.
- We do **not** use third-party advertising SDKs inside the app to profile you for ads.

If a specific build or distribution channel adds analytics or crash reporting, that channel’s terms and in-app notices apply in addition to this policy.

---

## 3. Data stored on your device

Subly stores the data you create (for example subscriptions, expenses, categories, tags, settings, and optional credentials for features you enable) **locally** on your device, using the storage mechanisms of that platform (such as application data directories or encrypted stores where the OS provides them).

You are responsible for the security of your device, backups, and any exports you create.

---

## 4. Optional features that use the network

When you turn on or use optional features, the app may connect to services **you** choose or configure. Examples include:

- **Cloud sync** — If you connect Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, WebDAV, or iCloud (where available), your sync payload is transmitted to that provider under **their** privacy policy and terms. Only the data required for that integration is sent. OAuth tokens and secrets are handled according to the platform’s secure storage APIs where applicable.
- **Exchange rates** — If you configure external rate providers or update currency data, requests go to those providers’ endpoints with the parameters you supply (such as API keys stored on your device).
- **Notifications** — Local or scheduled notifications use the operating system’s notification APIs; content is derived from data already on your device unless you configure a separate delivery channel.
- **Updates and distribution** — Installers, updates, or store downloads are delivered by the store or host you obtained the app from (for example GitHub Releases or an app marketplace), which has its own privacy practices.

We do not control third-party services. Please read their privacy policies before enabling integrations.

---

## 5. Open source and community

The Subly source code may be published in a public repository (for example on GitHub). **Issues, pull requests, discussions, and profile data** you submit there are governed by that platform’s privacy policy and visibility rules. Do not post secrets, API keys, or personal financial data in public threads.

---

## 6. Legal bases (EEA / UK users)

Where GDPR or similar law applies, we rely on:

- **Performance of a contract / steps at your request** — providing the app and features you enable;
- **Legitimate interests** — maintaining and improving the software, security, and support, where not overridden by your rights;
- **Consent** — where required for optional processing (for example certain integrations you explicitly activate).

---

## 7. Retention

Data you keep in the app remains on your device until you delete it, uninstall the app, or use built-in delete/export features. Data you upload to a third-party cloud stays subject to that provider’s retention and deletion tools.

We do not retain a copy of your full app database on our servers as part of normal Subly operation described in this policy.

---

## 8. Security

We design Subly to minimize unnecessary data exposure. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure. You should use device encryption, strong OS passwords, and reputable sync providers.

---

## 9. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of personal data, and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

For data held **only on your device**, exercising those rights is typically done by using app settings, export/delete features, or removing the app. For data held by **third parties** (cloud sync, stores, or GitHub), contact those parties directly.

---

## 10. Children’s privacy

Subly is not directed at children under the age where parental consent is required in your region. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children for marketing. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a channel we control, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

---

## 11. International transfers

If you use cloud services or stores outside your country, your information may be processed in other jurisdictions. Those providers’ terms apply.

---

## 12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective date” at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app or our published materials after an update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy, except where a stricter consent requirement applies under law.
